&nbsp;这为他的独裁统治奠定了坚实的基础。他要继续迫害新疆维吾尔族人，继续迫害那些敢于表达不同政治意见的异议人士，继续成就他的皇帝梦！习近平不但不承认清零政策给民生造成了惨不忍睹的人间悲剧，还在继续夸奖清零政策如何取得成功！习近平的道貌岸然和指鹿为马将会继续把中国民众带入到黑暗和偏执当中， 会培养出一个个小独裁者，成天梦想中国有多强大，美国如何针对中国。但是中国的普通大众普遍受了洗脑教育，认识不到共产党的独裁是为少数人服务的。中国人的机会和言论自由都是被共产党牢牢控制，死死掐住咽喉不得呼吸新鲜空气。很多底层的人特别像我父母一辈，他们生活过得不好只会觉得是命中注定，绝对不会想到这些都是因为共产党把所有的好机会都给切断给了那些少数人，普通人永远不会有好机会再努力也没有用。</p>  <p>&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;最近Tiktok的国会听证会上了就提到了，共产党可以随意接触到Tiktok在美国收集到的数据，而且Tiktok由于是北京字节跳动的子公司，很有可能会成为共产党的洗脑工具。共产党可以通过Tiktok向年轻人传达<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/forum2/topic6177.html" title="《共产主义的终极目的》" target="_blank">共产主义</a></span>的大谎言，让年轻人相信<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%85%b1%e4%ba%a7%e4%b8%bb%e4%b9%89/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 共产主义 下的日志">共产主义</a>比<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/forum2/topic920.html" title="资本主义与自由" target="_blank">资本主义</a></span>更好。那些还没有独立思考能力的年轻人是很容易被共产党蛊惑的。我自己就是一个很好的例子。我还在中国的时候不管共产党做了什么惨不忍睹的事情，只觉得是麻木了漠不关心或者就会怪受害人不好，而不会去反思共产党的没人道的独裁统治是错误的。如果没有铁链女的事件，让我接触到了不同的言论和共产党黑暗制度的实质，我到现在还是蒙在鼓里。</p> <p>&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;现在习近平连任了，我们<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e6%b0%91%e4%b8%bb%e5%85%9a/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 民主党 下的日志">民主党</a>一直在不停的在反抗共产党，以后的路也许就更难走了，对我们的迫害也会更变本加厉。所幸的是我们这在美国这个自由的国度里还可以继续我们的倡议和反抗<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/forum11/topic276.html" title="禁片：评中国共产党的暴政" target="_blank">暴政</a></span>！</p> <p>&nbsp; &nbsp;After a series of preparations and strategies, Xi Jinping finally took the throne again for the third time. On March 10, 2023, at the 3rd plenary meeting of the 14th National People&#39;s Congress of China, a total of 2,952 votes passed unanimously to elect the 69-year-old Xi Jinping as the re-elected president of the country and chairman of the Central Military Commission of China. Prior to this, Xi Jinping successfully re-elected as the general secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party(CCP) for the third time after the 20th Congress of the CCP. At that time, Xi Jinping once again gathered all the supreme power of the country in one person. And the so-called election is completely a formality. Xi Jinping has long been determined by himself to be elected as the third president of the country.</p>  <p>&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;This laid a solid foundation for his dictatorship. He will continue to persecute the Uighurs in Xinjiang, continue to persecute dissidents who dare to express different political opinions, and continue to fulfill his dream of being an emperor! Not only does Xi Jinping not admit that the zero covid policy has caused a terrible human tragedy to people&rsquo;s livelihood, but he continues to praise how the zero covid policy has succeeded! Xi Jinping&#39;s sanctimonious behavior will continue to lead the Chinese people into darkness and paranoia, and will cultivate small dictators who dream about how powerful China is and how the United States will target China. However, ordinary people in China have generally received brainwashing education, and they do not realize that the dictatorship of the CCP is only for the minority CCP members. Chinese people&#39;s opportunities and freedom of speech are firmly controlled by the CCP, choking their throats to prevent them from breathing fresh air. Many people at the bottom, especially like my parents&rsquo; generation, only feel that they are destined to live a bad life. They will never think that all these are because the CCP has cut off all good opportunities for those CCP members. Ordinary people will never have a good chance. No matter how hard you work, it is useless.</p> <p>&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;It was mentioned at the recent Tiktok congressional hearing that the CCP can freely access the data collected by Tiktok in the United States, and because Tiktok is a subsidiary of Beijing ByteDance, it is likely to become a brainwashing tool for the CCP. The CCP can convey the big lie of communism to young people through Tiktok, making young people believe that communism is better than capitalism. Those young people who do not yet have the ability to think independently are easily bewitched by the CCP&rsquo;s agenda. I am a good example of this myself. When I was still in China, no matter what horrible things the CCP did, I just felt numb and indifferent or blamed the victims, instead of reflecting on the wrongness of the CCP&rsquo;s inhuman dictatorship. If it weren&#39;t for the incident of the Chained Woman, I have been exposed to different opinions and the essence of the dark system of the CCP, and I am still in the dark.</p> <p>&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Now that Xi Jinping has been re-elected, our CDP has been constantly resisting the CCP&rsquo;s dictatorship and human rights abuse. The road ahead may be even more difficult, and the persecution of us will intensify. Fortunately, we can continue our initiative and resist tyranny in the free country of the United States!</p> <p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Li Cuiyun, March
