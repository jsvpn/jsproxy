<p>他并在文中批评“左胶”，指他本人努力想维护的<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e4%bf%9d%e5%ae%88%e4%b8%bb%e4%b9%89/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 保守主义
