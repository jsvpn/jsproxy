让仝卓获得应届生学籍。</p>  <p>仝卓引发的作弊争议, 让在山西临汾市<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e4%ba%ba%e5%a4%a7%e5%b8%b8%e5%a7%94/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 人大常委
