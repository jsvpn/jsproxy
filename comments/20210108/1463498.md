Like all the Americans I’m outraged by the violence, lawlessness, and mayhem.</p> <p>首先，我要谈谈（昨天）国会大厦发生的令人发指的（暴力）事件。和所有<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 美国人 下的日志">美国人</a>一样，我无法容忍暴力以及无法无天的混乱。</p> <p>I immediately deployed the Nation Guard and Federal Law Enforcement to secure the building, and expel the intruders.</p> <p>我当时立即调配了国民警卫队和联邦执法部门来保护大楼的安全，并驱逐<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E5%85%A5%E4%BE%B5%E8%80%85/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 入侵者 下的日志">入侵者</a>。</p> <p>America is and must always be a nation with law and order.</p> <p>美国永远是一个有法律和秩序的国家。</p> <p>The demonstrators who infiltrated the Capitol have defiled the seek of America democracy.</p> <p>那些闯入国会大厦的示威者们玷污了美国的民主。</p> <p>To those who engaged in the excess violence in destruction, you do not represent our country.</p> <p>那些暴力破坏的人，你们不代表我们的国家。</p> <p>And to those who broke the law, you will pay.</p> <p>而那些违法的人，你们将付出代价。</p>  <p>We have just been through a tense election.</p> <p>我们刚刚经历了一场激烈的<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e9%80%89%e4%b8%be/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 选举 下的日志">选举</a>。</p> <p>And the emotions are high.</p> <p>大家情绪激烈。</p> <p>But now the temperature must be cooled, and calm restored.</p> <p>但现在是（给激烈的情绪）降温的时候了，让我们恢复平静。</p> <p>We must get on the business of America.</p> <p>我们必须开始着手处理国家的事务了。</p> <p>My campaign vigorously pursue every legal avenue, to contest the election results.</p> <p>我的竞选团队积极寻求各种法律途径来质疑<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E9%80%89%E4%B8%BE%E7%BB%93%E6%9E%9C/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 选举结果 下的日志">选举结果</a>。</p> <p>My only goal was to insure the integrity of the votes.</p> <p>我唯一的目标就是确保选举的公平和公正。</p> <p>In so doing I was fighting to defend American Democracy.</p> <p>我是为了扞卫美国的民主制度。</p>  <p>I continue to strongly believe that we must reform our election laws, to verify the identity and eligibility of all voters, to insure faith and confidence in all future elections.</p> <p>我仍然坚信我们必须改革选举法。要核实所有选民的身份和资格，以确保美国人民对未来选举的信心。</p> <p>Now congress has certified the results.</p> <p>现在国会已经认证了本次选举结果。</p> <p>A new administration will be inaugurated on January 20th, my focus turns to insure a smooth, orderly and seamless transition power.</p> <p>新政府将于1月20日就职，我的工作重心将是确保平稳、有序和顺利的权力过渡。</p> <p>This moment calls for healing and reconciliation.</p> <p>此刻，我们需要的是彼此和解。</p> <p>2020 has been a challenges time for our people.</p> <p>2020年是极具挑战的一年。</p> <p>A maleficent pandemic has abandoned the lives of our citizens, isolated millions in their homes,  damaged our economy, and claim countless lives.</p> <p>一场如洪水猛兽般的大<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E6%B5%81%E8%A1%8C%E7%97%85/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 流行病 下的日志">流行病</a>毒夺走了重创了我们的生活、让数百万人隔离在家、破坏了我们的经济，并夺走了无数的生命。</p> <p>Defeating this pandemic and rebuilding the greatest economy on earth, we require all of us working together.</p> <p>要战胜这场大流行病、重建地球上最伟大的经济，就需要我们所有人共同努力。</p>  <p>It will require or renew our emphasis on the civic values, patriotism, faith, charity, community and family.</p> <p>现在需要我们重拾公民价值、爱国主义、信仰、慈善、社区和家庭。</p> <p>We must revive lights and secure bonds of love and loyalty that bond us together, as ONE national family.</p> <p>我们必须重新振作启来，用爱和忠诚把我们国家紧密结合在一起。</p> <p>To the citizen of our country, serving you as the president has been the honor of my lifetime.</p> <p>致美国公民，身为总统为你们服务是我一生的荣耀。</p> <p>And to my wonderful supporters, I know you are disappointed.</p> <p>致我那些非凡的支持者们，我知道你们感到失望。</p> <p>But I also want you to know, that our incredible journey is only just beginning.</p> <p>但我也想让你们知道，我们的旅程才刚刚开始。</p> <p>Thank you. God bless you. And God bless America.</p> <p>谢谢你们。愿上帝保佑你们。愿上帝保佑美利坚。</p> <p></p> <p>本文章或节目经希望之声编辑制作，转载请注明希望之声并包含原文标题及链接。</p>  <ul class='op-related-articles' title='相关阅读'> <li><a
