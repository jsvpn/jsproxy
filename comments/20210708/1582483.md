<p><figcaption>凉拌素什锦（网络图片）</figcaption></figure> <p>【<span class='wp_keywordlink_affiliate'><a href="https://www.soundofhope.org" title="希望之声" target="_blank">希望之声</a></span>2021年7月7日】（<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%b8%8c%e6%9c%9b%e4%b9%8b%e5%a3%b0/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 希望之声 下的日志">希望之声</a>记者<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%AA%9B%E5%AA%9B/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 李媛媛 下的日志">李媛媛</a>综合报导）今天的这道凉拌菜选用了非常普通的几种常见菜，配在一起，不但颜色漂亮，而且营养丰富。芹菜有降压降脂作用，清清脆脆；泡发的腐竹可以补钙，入口有嚼劲儿；<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e8%83%a1%e8%90%9d%e5%8d%9c/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 胡萝卜 下的日志">胡萝卜</a>对每天使用电脑和手机的人来说，可以帮助恢复<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e7%9c%bc%e7%96%b2%e5%8a%b3/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 眼疲劳 下的日志">眼疲劳</a>。<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E8%80%B3/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 木耳 下的日志">木耳</a>被人们称为清道夫，既可以清除体内的垃圾，还丰富了颜色。所以又可称为是一道清清爽爽的<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%85%bb%e7%94%9f/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 养生 下的日志">养生</a>菜。</p> <h3>食材：</h3> <p>木耳 10克，腐竹 2根，芹菜 2根，胡<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e8%90%9d%e5%8d%9c/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 萝卜 下的日志">萝卜</a> 1个，花生米 40克，香菜 2棵</p> <p><strong>调料：</strong></p> <p>盐 少许，红油 1茶匙，香油 1茶匙，糖 1茶匙</p>  <h3>做法：</h3> <p>1. 芹菜洗净切丝，胡萝卜去皮切条。</p> <p>2. <a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e9%bb%91%e6%9c%a8%e8%80%b3/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 黑木耳 下的日志">黑木耳</a>，腐竹提前泡发，木耳撕成小朵，腐竹切寸段。花生米最好提前用花椒大料煮熟。</p> <p>3. 锅中烧水，将木耳和腐竹放入煮2分钟焯水。用同一锅水再焯芹菜，胡萝卜。</p> <p>4. 将所有食材放入一个大碗中，放入适量的盐，红油，香油，糖搅拌均匀即可。</p>  <h3>Celery and Carrots Salad</h3> <h3>Ingredients:</h3> <p>10g black fungus, 2 Dried bean-curd stick, 2 celery sticks, 1 carrot, 40g peanuts, 2 coriander sticks</p> <p><strong>Seasoning:</strong></p> <p>A pinch of salt, 1 tsp red chilli oil, 1 tsp sesame oil, 1 tsp of sugar</p> <h3>Method:</h3> <p>1. wash the celery and cut into 1 inch long slices. Peel the carrot and cut into a inch long strips.</p>  <p>2. Black fungus and Dried bean-curd sticks need to soak in warm water in advance. After the fungus expanding, tear them into a small pieces. When the bean-curd become soft, cut into 5mm wide pieces. Put peanuts to a pan of boiling salted water with 1 star anise simmer for 2-3 minutes. </p> <p>3. Filling a large saucepan with plenty of water and bring to boil, put the fungus and bean-curd sticks in and cook for 2 minutes, then use a slotted spoon quickly transfer to a bowl full of cold water, then drain the cold water. </p> <p>4. Put the celery and carrots into the boiling water, cook for 2-3 minutes, then transfer to the cold water.</p> <p>5. Put all the ingredients in a large bowl, season with salt, red chilli oil, sesame oil, sugar and mix well.</p>  <p>本文章或节目经希望之声编辑制作，转载请注明希望之声并包含原文标题及链接。 </p> <ul class='op-related-articles' title='相关阅读'> <li><a href='https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/comments/20210707/1582410.html'
