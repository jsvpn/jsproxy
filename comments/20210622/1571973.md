href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e8%be%a3%e6%a4%92/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 辣椒 下的日志">辣椒</a> 2根，红辣椒 2根，姜 2片，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e7%94%9f%e6%8a%bd/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 生抽 下的日志">生抽</a> 1茶匙，水<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e6%b7%80%e7%b2%89/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 淀粉 下的日志">淀粉</a>适量，盐 少许</p>  <h3>做法：</h3> <ol> <li>将豆干洗净切成丝，装盘备用。青、红辣椒去籽切成丝，姜切丝。</li> <li>锅中放油，六成热时放入姜炒香，放入青、红辣椒丝炒20秒，然后倒入<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 豆腐 下的日志">豆腐</a>丝，加入适量盐，少许生抽、翻炒均匀，最后淋少许水淀粉勾芡，翻炒均匀即可。</li> </ol> <h3>Spicy Stir Fry Tofu Slices</h3> <p><strong>Ingredients:</strong></p> <p>150g  Five Spice Tofu, 2 green chilies, 2 red chilies, 2 slices of ginger, 1 tsp light soy sauce, 2 tsp corn flour + water, a pinch salt</p>  <p><strong>Method</strong>:</p> <p>1. Cut the Five Spicy Tofu, red and green chillies and ginger into slices.</p>  <p>2. Heat a frying pan with 2 tbsp oil, put ginger in fry for 20 seconds, add green and red chillies, stir fry for 30 seconds, then put all the spicy tofu slices into the pan, add a pinch of salt, 1 tsp light soy sauce fry for 30 seconds, then pour the corn flour water and give a quick stir and mix well.</p> <p>本文章或节目经希望之声编辑制作，转载请注明希望之声并包含原文标题及链接。 </p>  <ul class='op-related-articles' title='相关阅读'>
