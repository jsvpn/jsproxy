href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%b8%8c%e6%9c%9b%e4%b9%8b%e5%a3%b0/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 希望之声 下的日志">希望之声</a>记者<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%AA%9B%E5%AA%9B/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 李媛媛 下的日志">李媛媛</a>综合报导）我们这道擂椒茄子与其它凉拌茄子不同的是我们没有将茄子完全撕开，而是保持了茄子的完整性。这样可以最大程度的保留茄子里面所有的营养，再趁热淋上各种<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E8%B0%83%E5%91%B3%E6%96%99/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 调味料 下的日志">调味料</a>，使得这道<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e6%96%99%e7%90%86/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 料理 下的日志">料理</a>非常的别具一格。这道菜的关键是擂椒酱，也就是把青红<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e8%be%a3%e6%a4%92/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 辣椒 下的日志">辣椒</a>、蒜捣成泥茸状，用热油浇在上面激发出小料的香气，然后淋在茄子上。</p> <h3>材料：</h3> <p>长茄子1个，红辣椒2个，青尖椒3个，蒜4个，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8F%9C/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 香菜 下的日志">香菜</a>少量。</p> <h3>调料：</h3> <p>盐2克，糖3克，2勺蒸鱼豉油，1勺<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E8%9A%9D%E6%B2%B9/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 蚝油 下的日志">蚝油</a>，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e6%a9%84%e6%a6%84%e6%b2%b9/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 橄榄油 下的日志">橄榄油</a>10克，花椒适量。</p> <h3>做法：</h3> <p><strong>1. 准备工作：</strong>青红辣椒切片，蒜切碎。茄子洗净去蒂，交叉切十字刀，不要切断底部（见下图）。香菜洗净切碎。</p>  <figure><figcaption><strong>茄子切十字刀（网络图片）</strong></figcaption></figure> <p><strong>2. 蒸茄子：</strong>蒸锅烧水，将茄子放入蒸锅蒸20分钟 。</p> <p><strong>3. 准备料汁：</strong>青红辣椒切碎后和蒜一同放入碗中，加入蒸鱼豉油、盐、糖、蚝油。</p> <p><strong>4. 炸花椒油：</strong>锅中加入橄榄油，放1茶匙花椒，小火慢炸至花椒变成深棕色，将热油趁热隔筛网倒入辣椒泥中，搅匀即成擂椒酱。</p> <p><strong>5. 成菜：</strong>茄子蒸好后取出，沥掉茄子盘中的汤，茄子晾凉，将擂椒酱倒在茄子上面，撒香菜即可。</p>  <h3>Aubergine Salad</h3> <h3>Ingredients:</h3> <p>1 long aubergine, 2 red chilli, 3 green chilli, 4 cloves garlic, 1 coriander.</p> <p><strong>Seasoning:</strong></p> <p>a pinch salt, 3g sugar, 2 tbsp seasoned soy sauce for seafood, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, 2 tbsp olive oil,  1 tsp peppercorn.</p> <h3>Method:</h3> <p>1. Cut the green and red chilli into slices, chop the garlic. Wash the aubergine and trim the top and bottom, cut the aubergine in cross lines with a knife, but do not cut all the way through (see the picture above). </p>  <p>2.Put the aubergine on a plate and put in a steamer, steam for 20 minutes.</p> <p>3. Put the green and red chilli, garlic in a bowl, then add salt, sugar, seasoned soy sauce for seafood, oyster sauce in the bowl.</p> <p>4. Heat a frying pan with 2 tbsp olive oil, put 1 tsp of peppercorn into oil fry on low heat until the peppercorns turns dark brown, and pour the hot oil into the bowl and stir well.</p> <p>5. Take the aubergine out of the steamer, drain the water from the plate, leave the aubergine cool, then pour the chili sauce on top of the aubergine, garnish with coriander.</p>
