<p>作者：<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%bb%96%e4%ba%a6%e6%ad%a6/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 廖亦武 下的日志">廖亦武</a></p> <p>2022年11月2日，新疆<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E4%B9%8C%E9%B2%81%E6%9C%A8%E9%BD%90/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 乌鲁木齐 下的日志">乌鲁木齐</a>市中心吉祥苑社区，因防疫封城一百多天，导致高楼火灾，却无法<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/forum5/topic38.html" title="劫难逃生有秘诀" target="_blank">逃生</a></span>和援救。单元门从外面紧锁，在楼道外面，还竖立著防疫栅栏和金属挡牆，所以，当消防人员随车赶到，也突不破障碍。据<span class='wp_keywordlink_affiliate'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/" title="中国" target="_blank">中国</a></span>官方公佈，有十人被活活烧死，九人重伤。可同一居民楼的倖存者们证实，至少有四十人葬身火海。</p> <p>一位叫Bahargul的维吾尔族遇难者，人生的最后一则微信<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tougao/" title="留言" target="_blank">留言</a></span>是：“这裡现在严重缺氧，火著的越来越大．．．单元门从外面锁著，我们出不去．．．此时此刻，我已经不指望什麽了，只想在这裡发声，让更多的人知道发生了什麽。”</p> <p>通过社交网路，这起惨剧转眼在全国疯传。但是从2020年1月23日武汉封城始，由伟大、光荣、正确的病毒皇帝<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e4%b9%a0%e8%bf%91%e5%b9%b3/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 习近平 下的日志">习近平</a>所开创的“全民核酸，动态清零”模式，必须毫不动摇地贯彻到底。所以，网警们一如既往，疯狗般见此内容就删帖，就警告，就设置“敏感词”。就追查“健康码”并上门抓人。但是人类不是一动不动的木头，11月25日，愤怒到极点的乌鲁木齐数万市民突破社区封锁，在市政府大门聚集，要求结束封城清零。 喊出“不自由，毋宁死”的口号——通过现场无数手机拍摄的视屏， 和头天发生的惨剧一块，再次在全国疯传。</p> <p>再接著，11月26，也就是乌鲁木齐市民抗议集会次日，一个黑衣黑口罩的<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%a5%b3%e7%94%9f/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 女生 下的日志">女生</a>，　双手将一张白纸举在胸前，　静静地伫立在南京传媒大学校园中心的鼓楼阶梯下。寒风阵阵，她却如雕像一动不动。一会儿，女生的政治辅导员跑过来，抽走了女生手中的白纸。有路人质问：“白纸没有攻击力，你为什要拿走？”</p> <p>女生依旧如雕像一动不动，双手举著一张莫须有的“白纸”。在场的所有人都看懂了，于是从教室、寝室、楼道、四面八方，推倒一道道防疫围栏，陆陆续续走过来。胸前举白纸的，女生居多，然后才是反应稍慢的男生。</p> <p>夜幕渐渐降临，举白纸的人已经数不清了。有一个男生来到第一个举白纸的女生跟前，深深鞠躬，然后大声说：“感谢你给了我勇气！我是新疆人，本来我很害怕，但是现在我要为我的家乡，为我那些死于防疫火灾的同胞大声说话。愿死者安息！愿生者自由！”</p> <p>校园内的“非法群聚”惊动了辅导员和校长，他们赶过来规劝大伙儿解散。校长说：“你们要为今天的行为付出代价！”这只能让群情更加激愤。这时候，大批武警部队已经抵达了大学校门。　与此同时，现场手机视屏和文字传遍了全国。上海、西安、成都、广州、北京等50多个大学，不计其数的大学生推倒连绵不绝的防疫围栏和挡板，突破大白们的封控，到校园中心“非法群聚”。他们都举著白纸，悼念乌鲁木齐的死者，他们心裡都清楚——这三年的“全民核酸，动态清零”的国策，最初的试验地就是新疆无数个维吾尔族“再教育集中营”。后来，在新疆的领先于全世界的全面监控的“成功经验”，藉口“防疫”，演变成全体中国人必须接受的公开的“健康码制度”。任何个人的任何行动，都取决于扫码的结果——红、黄、绿——你的身体和大脑的本能讯息，即刻就被国家机器所掌握，并进行自动分析和处理。</p> <p>就这样，Covid-19经过三年遗传和演变，不是致死率大幅度降低的Omicron（奥密克戎），　而成为covid－1984——这是从古至今，从未有过的科技独裁模式，通过武汉病毒和互联网的狼狈为奸，习近平帝国终于实现了防火牆内的“<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/forum2/topic6177.html" title="《共产主义的终极目的》" target="_blank">共产主义</a></span>健康码制度”，也就是以防疫为藉口的全民监控制度。如果你拒不接受，就无法在中国生存。</p>  <p>我想，如果乔治．奥威尔还健在，定会重写<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/forum2/topic186.html" title="乔治.奥威尔《1984》" target="_blank">《1984》</a></span>。</p> <p>但是，双手举白纸的女生出现了。成千上万的举白纸的人民出现了。谁能把白纸“动态清零”？虽然，已经有大量出售白纸的商店和公司接到警方电话，在网上发佈配合政府，不出售白纸的“爱国公告”，但是不说话的白纸，人人都能看懂和听懂：</p> <p>我知道你们不准我说话</p> <p>但是我要告诉所有人</p> <p>你们不准我说话</p> <p>我要说</p> <p>你们不准我说</p> <p>这就是<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e7%99%bd%e7%ba%b8%e9%9d%a9%e5%91%bd/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 白纸革命 下的日志">白纸革命</a>。沉默的行为艺术的<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e9%9d%a9%e5%91%bd/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 革命 下的日志">革命</a>。</p> <p>当白纸飞抵北京，在清华大学，数百理<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/forum11/topic309.html" title="禁片：“科学”的棍子" target="_blank">科学</a></span>生在白纸上添加了英文物理公式，然后才一起举起来抗议。这个公式是著名广义相对论宇宙模型的弗裡德曼方程式Friedman （和自由发音相近）Equation的一部分，被省去的方程最后一项，是来自暗能量的贡献，　第二项k不为零，则代表开放性宇宙。其寓意是：自由，开放，不要黑势力！</p> <p>到了2022年 11月26日傍晚，来自东西南北的上海市民举著白纸，突破警方重重封锁，在乌鲁木齐中路集会，纪念死难同胞，成千上万人齐声怒吼：“<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%85%b1%e4%ba%a7%e5%85%9a/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 共产党 下的日志">共产党</a>下台！　<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e4%b9%a0%e8%bf%91%e5%b9%b3%e4%b8%8b%e5%8f%b0/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 习近平下台 下的日志">习近平下台</a>！”</p> <p><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%85%b1%e4%ba%a7%e5%85%9a%e4%b8%8b%e5%8f%b0/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 共产党下台 下的日志">共产党下台</a>——历史将记住这前所未有的怒吼，沉默的白纸引发的中国人民的怒吼——1949年以降，亿万人惨死于<span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/forum11/topic276.html" title="禁片：评中国共产党的暴政" target="_blank">暴政</a></span>，也未有过； 1989年6月，3000多人惨死于屠城，也未有过。</p>  <p>２０２２年１１月２８日</p> <p>The Blank Paper Revolution</p> <p>History will Remember this Unprecedented Shout of Anger</p> <p>Liao Yiwu（Translated by Michael M. Day）</p> <p>On November 24, 2022, a fire broke out in a high-rise building in the Jixiangyuan neighborhood of downtown Urumqi, Xinjiang. The city had already been closed for over 100 days to prevent the spread of the epidemic. Sealing off their building left no way for the people inside to escape. The door of their housing units had been locked from the outside. Outside the building was an epidemic control fence and a metal retaining wall. When firefighters in their fire trucks, they were unable to break through the barrier. According to the Chinese official announcement, ten people were burned alive and nine others were seriously injured. Survivors of that same building confirmed however that at least 40 people died in the fire.</p> <p>The last WeChat message of a Uighur victim of the fire named Bahargul read as follows: “There’s a serious lack of oxygen right now. The fire is growing bigger and bigger…. The apartment unit door is locked from the outside, we can’t get out…. At this moment, I’ve no real hopes, I only want to say something here to let more people know what’s happened.”</p> <p>Word of this tragic event went viral across the country by way of social networks on the Internet. However, ever since the city of Wuhan was sealed off on the 23rd of January 2020, the “nucleic acid [tests] for all until COVID is zeroed out” model initiated by the great, glorious, and correct emperor of COVID, Xi Jinping, must be unwaveringly carried through to the end. So, as per usual, the Internet police were whipped into a frenzy deleting messages, sending out warnings, and setting new “sensitive words” to be censored as soon as they caught a glimpse of this content. They tracked down the individual [computer and smart phone] “health codes” and came to the door to make arrests. But humans are not inert logs, and on November 25th, thousands of enraged citizens of Urumqi burst through district quarantines and gathered at the gates of the municipal government demanding an end to the closure of the city and the clearance of COVID. Shouting “give me liberty or give me death”, the videos shot by countless mobile phones on the scene, together with the tragedy that happened the day before, once again went viral across the country.</p> <p>Then, on November 26, the day after Urumqi citizens rallied to protest, a female student dressed in black wearing a black mask held up a piece of blank white paper in front of her chest and stood silently before the steps of the Drum Tower at the center of the Nanjing Communication University campus. A cold wind was blowing, but she remained motionless as a statue. After a while, a political counselor ran over and took the blank paper from her hands. A passer-by asked, &#8220;Blank paper is no threat. Why take it away?&#8221;</p> <p>The student remained motionless, holding the superfluous &#8220;blank paper&#8221; in both her hands. Yet everybody present understood its message, and so came others from classrooms, dormitories, corridors, from in all directions, knocking down epidemic prevention fences on their way. Of those holding sheets of blank paper in front of their chests, most were female, the males seemingly somewhat slower to respond.</p> <p>As night fell, there were already countless people holding up blank sheets of paper. A male student came up to the first student, bowed deeply, and said loudly: &#8220;Thank you for giving me courage! I am from Xinjiang. I was very scared at first, but now I want to fight for my homeland, to speak loudly for my compatriots who died in the epidemic prevention fire. May the dead rest in peace! May the living be free!&#8221;</p> <p>The &#8220;illegal gathering&#8221; on the campus alarmed the political counselors and the university chancellor, and they now rushed up to persuade everyone to leave. The chancellor said, &#8220;You will pay a price for what you do today!&#8221; which only further fueled the anger. Now, a large troop of armed police arrived at the main university gate. At the same time, however, mobile phone video and text of the scene was spreading all over the country. In more than 50 universities in Shanghai, Xi&#8217;an, Chengdu, Guangzhou, Beijing, etc., countless college students tore down the endless anti-epidemic fences and barricades, broke through the cordon of the white-smocked COVID officers, and &#8220;illegally gathered&#8221; in the center of their campuses. They all held up blank sheets of paper to commemorate the dead in Urumqi, all clear in their hearts that for the past three years the national policy of “nucleic acid [tests] for all until COVID is zeroed out” was initially tested in countless Uyghur &#8220;re-education concentration camps&#8221; in Xinjiang. Later, the world-leading &#8220;successful experience&#8221; of Xinjiang&#8217;s comprehensive surveillance evolved into an open &#8220;health code system&#8221; that all Chinese must accept during unending epidemic prevention. Any action of any individual depends on the result of scanning this code &#8211; red, yellow, or green – containing basic information about your body and brain, which is immediately seized by the state machine and automatically analyzed and processed &#8211; I think if George Orwell were still alive, he would certainly rewrite his novel, 1984.</p>  <p>That&#8217;s it, The Wuhan virus (COVID-19) has undergone a three-year mutation, and instead of the Omicron with which all mankind is to coexist, it is COVID-1984 &#8211; a kind of high tech dictatorship never before seen, the monstrous spawn of the Wuhan virus and Internet technologies. The Xi Jinping empire has finally achieved a &#8220;communist health code system&#8221; within the Great Red Firewall – a system of universal surveillance formed ostensibly for epidemic control. If you reject it, you cannot survive in China.</p> <p>However, the female student holding a sheet of blank paper in both hands emerged. Then thousands of people holding sheets of blank paper appeared. Who can &#8220;zero out&#8221; sheets of blank paper? Even though a large number of shops and companies that sell blank paper received calls from the police and a &#8220;patriotic announcement&#8221; was published on the Internet to cooperate with the government and not sell blank paper, everyone understands a speechless sheet of blank paper:</p> <p>I know you don’t permit me speech</p> <p>But I want to tell everyone</p> <p>If you don’t permit me speech</p> <p>I will say</p> <p>What you do not permit me</p> <p>This is a blank paper revolution. Revolution as silent performance art.</p> <p>When the blank paper arrived in Beijing, at Tsinghua University, hundreds of science students added a physics formula to the white paper, and then they all raised the papers in their hands to protest. This formula is a part of the Friedman Equation, a dynamic equation for the expanding universe. The omitted last term of the equation is the contribution from dark energy; and the second term, k, is not zero, but rather represents an open universe, implying: freedom, openness, no black forces!</p> <p>On the evening of November 26, 2022, Shanghai citizens in the east, west, north, and south of the city, holding sheets of blank paper, broke through the heavy police blockades and gathered on Urumqi Middle Road to commemorate their dead compatriots. Thousands of people shouted in unison: &#8220;Communist Party step down! Xi Jinping step down! &#8221;</p> <p>Communist Party step down from the stage of history. And history will remember this unprecedented roar, a roar from the people of China triggered by a silent sheet of blank paper. Since 1949, millions of people have died tragically due to their tyranny on a scale that had never before occurred in the long history of the culture; and in June 1989, more than 3,000 people died tragically in a massacre in Beijing that was similarly unprecedented.</p>  <!--<div id="taboola-mid-1"></div>--><ul class='op-related-articles' title='相关阅读'> <li><a
