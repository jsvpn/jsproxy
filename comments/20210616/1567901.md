href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E8%B1%86%E7%9A%AE/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 豆皮 下的日志">豆皮</a>含有的优质大豆蛋白也是具有多种保健和<a class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 萝卜 下的日志">萝卜</a>更不用说了，是另一种物美价廉的小人参。黄瓜可以补充夏季人体消耗的水分，并且口感清爽。</p> <h3>食材：</h3> <p>豆皮 1张、黄瓜 1根、<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e8%83%a1%e8%90%9d%e5%8d%9c/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 胡萝卜 下的日志">胡萝卜</a> 1个、<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%a4%a7%e8%92%9c/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 大蒜 下的日志">大蒜</a> 3瓣、干<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e8%be%a3%e6%a4%92/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 辣椒 下的日志">辣椒</a> 1个、花椒 1汤匙，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e7%94%9f%e6%8a%bd/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 生抽 下的日志">生抽</a> 2汤匙、盐 少许、醋 1汤匙</p> <h3>做法：</h3> <p>1. 将豆皮、黄瓜、胡萝卜，分别切成细丝；干辣椒和大蒜切好备用。</p>  <p>2. 锅中烧水，放入豆皮、胡萝卜丝，分别焯水1分钟后捞出。然后立即放入冷水中，过凉后，捞出控干。</p> <p>3. 锅里烧热油，放入花椒小火慢慢炸香，花椒变深棕色后捞出。再放入大蒜、干辣椒小火慢慢炸出香味。</p> <p>4. 将炸好的调料倒入大碗中，加适量的生抽、盐、醋，与三丝一起搅拌均匀即可。</p>  <h3>Cucumber and Soybean Sheets Salad</h3> <h3>Ingredients:</h3> <p>1 slice Soybean sheet, 1 cucumber, 1 carrot, 3 cloves garlic, 1 dried chili, 1 tbsp Szechun peppercorns, 2 tbsp  light soy sauce, a pinch salt, 1 tbsp vinegar</p> <h3>Method:</h3> <p>1. Cut the soybean sheet, cucumber, and carrot into thin strips; cut dried chili to small pieces, chop the garlic.</p> <p>2. Boil water in a pot, add soybean sheet, carrot cook for a minute, then take them out, transfer into cold water immediately, then drain the cold water. </p>  <p>3. Heat a pan with 2 tbsp oil, add Szechun peppercorns into the oil fry on low heat, until the peppercorn turns dark brown and take them out. Use the same oil add garlic and dried chilies fry over the low heat for a minute. </p> <p>4. Pour the fried oil with garlic and chili on the vegetables, season with light soy sauce, salt, and vinegar, and mix well. </p> <p>本文章或节目经希望之声编辑制作，转载请注明希望之声并包含原文标题及链接。 </p>  <ul
