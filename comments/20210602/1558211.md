class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 老抽 下的日志">老抽</a>1/2汤匙、水<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e6%b7%80%e7%b2%89/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 淀粉 下的日志">淀粉</a>适量</p> <h3>做法：</h3> <p>1. 准备工作：茄子和尖椒去蒂，切条，然后将茄子放在盆中，加少许盐，拌匀，腌制10分钟，腌制出水，这样炒制时茄子就不会吸收太多的油，也很容易炒制</p>  <p>2.腌制猪肉：将猪肉切成丝后放入碗中，加入1茶匙生抽，1茶匙蚝油，1茶匙糖拌匀，1汤匙水，然后再加入1汤匙淀粉用手抓匀备用。</p> <p>3.锅加油烧热，下入茄条煸炒，将茄条炒制稍变软，盛出待用.</p> <p>4.用同一个锅，下入葱花，姜末炒香，再下入肉丝翻炒，炒制肉丝颜色变白，淋入料酒煸炒1分钟。下入茄条翻炒均匀，然后转中小火烧制入味。炒至断生时，加入尖椒丝和蒜末翻炒均匀，淋入适量水淀粉勾芡，加热至汤汁变得粘稠，芡汁都包裹在食材上即可。</p>  <h3>Quick Stir Fry Aubergine and Green Pepper</h3> <h3>Ingredients:</h3> <p>2 aubergine, 2 green peppers, 100g pork, 5 tbsp oil , 1 shallot, 3 slices ginger, 2 cloves of garlic, 1 tbsp light soy sauce, 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce, 1 tbsp Corn flour+ 3 tbsp water</p> <h3>Method:</h3> <p>1. Clean the vegetables and remove the stems of the aubergines and peppers, cut into strips. Then put the aubergines strips in a basin, add a little salt and mix well, marinate for 10 minutes. so that the aubergines will not absorb too much oil during cooking.</p> <p>2. Cut the pork into thin strips, and put it in a bowl, add 1 tsp of light soy sauce, 1 tsp of oyster sauce, 1 tsp of sugar and mix well, 1 tablespoon of water, and then add 1 tablespoon of starch.</p>  <p>3. Heat a frying pan with 5-6 tbsp oil, put the aubergine strips in the pan and stir fry until aubergines become slightly soft, take it out and set aside.</p> <p>4. In the same pan, put the onion, minced ginger in the pan fry for a minute, then add the pork and stir fry for 2 minutes, add 1 tbsp of cooking wine and stir fry for 1 minute. Then add the aubergines strips and stir-fry over a low heat, then add pepper and minced garlic, give a quick stir-fry, put the mixed water starch carry on to stir fry for a minute.</p>
