class='wp_keywordlink_affiliate'><a href="https://www.soundofhope.org" title="希望之声" target="_blank">希望之声</a></span>2021年6月3日】夏天没有食欲，来一碗这样的凉拌黄瓜，爽口开胃。里面加了木耳和腐竹，营养也很丰富，不喜欢吃辣的可以不加辣椒。</p> <h3><a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E6%9D%90/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 食材 下的日志">食材</a>：</h3> <p>黄瓜2根, 腐竹2根, 木耳8朵，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e8%8a%b1%e7%94%9f/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 花生 下的日志">花生</a>米 20克，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e5%a4%a7%e8%92%9c/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 大蒜 下的日志">大蒜</a>2瓣，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%B1%B3/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 小米 下的日志">小米</a>辣 2个，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e7%94%9f%e6%8a%bd/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 生抽 下的日志">生抽</a>2勺，陈醋1勺， 盐 少许, </p> <h3>做法：</h3> <p>1. 先把黄瓜洗净，拍扁然后切成小段，放在碗中；</p> <p>2. 腐竹提前用清水泡软，切成小段备用；木耳提前用清水浸泡30分钟；</p>  <figure><figcaption>木耳腐竹提前泡软（网络图片）</figcaption></figure> <p>3. 先把木耳放在开水里焯水2分钟左右，再把腐竹加进去焯水30秒，捞出来过下凉水，</p> <p>4. 锅中加入<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9%E6%B2%B9/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 植物油 下的日志">植物油</a>，冷油加入1把花生米，小火慢慢翻炒，翻炒至颜色变深，盛出备用。</p> <p>5. 2个小米辣切碎，大蒜切成蒜末，装在碗中，油锅里再加入适量的八角花椒，炒出香味后，把八角花椒捞出，再把热油直接浇在蒜末上，激发出香味，碗中加入2勺生抽、1勺米醋、1勺盐、1小勺白糖、搅拌均匀，料汁就调好了。</p> <p>6. 把沥干水分的木耳和腐竹根黄瓜放在一起，再把花生米也加进去，适量的香菜；</p>  <figure><figcaption>将所有食材放入碗中（网络图片）</figcaption></figure> <p>7. 最后把酱汁倒进去，搅拌均匀即可。</p> <h3>Cucumber Beancurd Stick Fungus Salad</h3> <h3>Ingredients:</h3> <p>2 cucumbers, 2 Beancurd Sticks , 8 fungus, 20g peanuts, 2 cloves garlic, 2 fresh red chilli, 2 tbsp light soy sauce, 1 tbsp vinegar, a pinch of salt</p> <h3>Method:</h3> <p>1. Wash the cucumbers, smash them flat, then cut into one inch long pieces, put in a bowl.</p> <p>2. The Beancurd Stick soak in warm water overnight, and cut into a inch long pieces. Black fungus is soaked in boil water for 30 minutes, then wash thoroughly.</p>  <p>3. Bring water to boil in a pan, put the fungus in boiling water for 30 seconds, then add the Beancurd pieces continue to boil for 30 seconds. Drain the water and put beancurd and black fungu into cold water.</p> <p>4. Heat a frying pan with 1tbsp oil, add peanut in the pan, fry over low heat until colour become light brown, then take them out and put in a bowl.</p> <p>5. cut 2 fresh red chilli into small pieces, chopped garlic and put chilli and garlic in a bowl, heap a frying pan with 2 tbsp oil until the oil is very hot, then pour the hot oil directly on the minced garlic. Then add 2 tbsp light soy sauce, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, a pinch of salt, 1 tsp white sugar to the bowl, mix well. Set the sauce aside.</p> <p>6. Put the fungus, beancurd and cucumber in a bowl, then add peanuts and coriander in the bowl, pour the sauce in the bowl mix well.</p>  <p>本文章或节目经<a
